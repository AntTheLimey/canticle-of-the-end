Physical Description

A timid, bespectacled young man of thirty-one whose nervous disposition is apparent in his every gesture. He carries himself with the hunched posture of a career desk officer.

Background

Lieutenant Orville is a clerk at the Admiralty in London. Under the influence of the Air Loom, he was compelled to create false service records for Captain_King, providing the documentation that supports King’s masquerade as a Royal Navy captain. Orville has no memory of producing the forgeries and would be horrified to learn of his involvement.
