Overview

A fashionable dressmaker’s shop owned and run by Madame_Beauregard, a sharp-witted Frenchwoman who has made herself indispensable to Brighton’s elite. Her establishment dresses the town’s most fashionable women and serves as a discreet venue for social intelligence — Madame Beauregard sees and remembers everything that passes through her fitting rooms. Her French refinement and professional discretion give her a particular authority among her clientele.

Campaign Significance

A social access point during Chapter 0.1, where the investigators could gather intelligence through the trusted privacy of a dressmaker’s appointment. Remains available as a resource if the party returns to Brighton.
