Overview

Walter Baines is a gruff veteran of the cult’s operations. He has prepared victims for sacrifice and is loyal to Dr. Hume without reservation. He serves as a primary guard in the sub-basement detention area.

Description

Gruff and weathered, Baines carries himself with military bearing. Sweat-dampened and stern.

Personality & Traits

  • Loyal and unquestioning; carries out orders without hesitation
  • Resentful of perceived weakness or defiance
  • Sadistic in his cruelty but not creative; follows Hume’s instructions precisely

Roleplaying Hooks

  • Will not hesitate to use violence
  • Cannot be reasoned with or intimidated
  • Responds only to direct orders from cult hierarchy
  • If Hume is killed or removed, may become confused/disoriented

Relationship to Campaign

Baines is a combat encounter NPC representing cultist muscle. He serves as an obstacle to prisoner rescue and may be defeated, captured, or killed during infiltration attempts.
