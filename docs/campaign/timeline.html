Timeline

[!info] How this file works This is the canonical record of collapsed reality — what actually happened in-game, in chronological order, grouped by chapter. Per the session-lifecycle skill convention: append-only. Past entries are not reorganised or edited once the session is wrapped up. Corrections go in a Canon Corrections section at the bottom with a date stamp.

Dates are in-game. Out-of-table session dates (e.g. “played on 2026-02-05”) are not recorded here — those live in session frontmatter.

The timeline is a reference card, not a recap. Each entry is terse. Wiki-links point to the full entity and session files that hold the narrative weight.
