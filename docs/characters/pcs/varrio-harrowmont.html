Varrio Harrowmont

Disrupted Venice cell off-camera, PTSD from witnessing Moreau's death, Fainted at sight of Nightgaunt, Killed Brenner after Engine revelation, Extracted from captivity via Dr. Fischbein's legal intervention

Player Phil Background Order of St. Aelfric consulting surgeon Status alive
Background Attributes Skills Current Status Key Moments Connections

Varrio Harrowmont (Dr. Septimus Harrowmont) is an Order of St. Aelfric consulting surgeon. He disrupted the Venice cell (Confraternita del Bel Canto) off-camera during Chapter 2.5 before joining the main party in Vienna.

Character statistics not yet recorded.

  • Surgical and anatomical expertise
  • Counter-surveillance awareness
  • Improvised weapons and fire doctrine (from the Masquerade Wächter encounters)

At Thaliastraße 12 as of dawn, 9 August 1814. Physically uninjured. Carried the heaviest composite operational load through Session 9 — extraction, courtyard, carriage, safehouse, cellar. Discovered the garret observation post.

Varrio was introduced to the table during Vienna Chapter 3, Session 2 onward. He has experienced significant trauma:

  • Session 5: Captured in street ambush near the Graben; witnessed Colonel Moreau’s assassination by the Bauer brothers
  • Session 6: Witnessed the Nightgaunt attack; fainted at the sight of the creature due to PTSD
  • Interrogation & Release: Captured during Vienna operations; extracted by Dr. Leopold Fischbein’s legal intervention on the Order’s behalf
  • Venice (Off-Camera): Successfully disrupted the Confraternita del Bel Canto cell from within
  • The Harmonic Engine Revelation (Session 2): Killed Brenner after the cultist revealed details of the Engine
  • Trauma & PTSD: Suffering ongoing psychological effects from witnessing Moreau’s death
  • Masquerade Ball Session 9 — Fire doctrine established: Built an improvised torch from a chair leg, tablecloth, and cognac. Thrust it into a Wächter’s jaw as the creature leapt at him — the Wächter ignited instantly and burned to death on the ballroom floor. Stepped on Adler’s wrist and pressed the torch to his hand until the tuning fork dropped. Set a second Wächter on fire in the courtyard (unaware Georgiana had psychically forced it to sit). Navigated the palace gardens to find Charles and the carriage. Explored the safehouse garret and discovered the observation post and rooftop escape hatch.
  • Order Operative: Full member, consulting surgeon
  • Medical Expertise: Surgical and anatomical knowledge crucial for understanding the Harmonic Engine
  • Legal Asset: Dr. Fischbein successfully leveraged Viennese law to extract him from police custody
  • Fire doctrine: Varrio’s torch went from desperate gamble to mission doctrine in two rounds. Fire is the Wächter’s hard counter.
