Emma Wentworth

Emma Wentworth is a 17-year-old gentlewoman from Tarryford, Wiltshire. She first joined the campaign during The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1, Autumn 1813) at Northlake Hall, where she and her sister Georgiana were locals attending the autumn ball. She became theatre-obsessed following the Drury Lane incident in London and is known for her courage and curiosity.

Emma remains active but injured. During the Nightgaunt attack at Palais Kinsky (Session 6), she demonstrated remarkable bravery by biting the creature’s neck, tearing away flesh. This act had unexpected consequences — Thomas Wyndham has become devoted to her protection, and Graf Maximilian von Sternberg has begun a formal romantic pursuit.

At the Grand Masquerade / Wächter incursion (Session 8–9), Emma sustained a knife wound beneath the arm during the corridor cluster extraction — damage sufficient to meet the CoC 7e Major Wound threshold (more than half HP in a single strike). Georgiana field-dressed the wound in the carriage en route to Thaliastraße 12. As of dawn, 9 August 1814, Emma is at the safehouse, stabilised, mobile but restricted — a Major Wound imposes a bonus die on all First Aid/Medicine rolls to recover and requires rest. Physical activity carries a risk of the wound reopening.
