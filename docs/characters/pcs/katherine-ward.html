Katherine_Ward

Trained thief and infiltrator, Moral code (no harm to innocents, no children, violence only in self-defense), Encountered and contained Hound of Tindalos, Recruited by Order after St. Margaret's Academy incident

Player Juel Points 200/200 Background Spy / Order operative / Companion/Chaperone Status alive
Background Cover Identity Current Status (Vienna, August 1814) Background: The Hound of Tindalos Professional Mentor Moral Code Skills Masquerade Ball — Session 9 Current Status (dawn, 9 August 1814)

Katherine Ward is the real name and alias “the Rook” — a trained spy and thief working for the Order of St. Aelfric. Her actual name is Katherine Langley. She was born into lower-class circumstances but received education through charity.

Mrs. Katherine Ward, widow of Captain Edward Ward (Royal Navy). Works professionally as a companion and chaperone for young ladies, which provides cover for her movements and access to society circles.

Katherine was dispatched directly to Vienna by Lord Harcourt after the party sent too many notes requesting support. She was introduced to the party at Session 7 and has replaced Charlotte Thorne as the active Order operative in the field.

Before joining the active party, Katherine encountered a Hound of Tindalos at St. Margaret’s Academy. She successfully contained the creature by disrupting angles — preventing its multi-dimensional pursuit. This encounter brought her to the Order’s attention and led to her recruitment.

Jonathan Pike (“Jon”), a locksmith-turned-broker, mentors Katherine and manages her professional identity. He is a trusted contact outside the Order structure.

Katherine operates under strict ethical constraints:

  • Will not harm innocents
  • Will not accept jobs involving children
  • Will not engage in violence except in self-defense
  • Professional restraint even in dangerous situations
  • Trained lockpick and infiltrator
  • Extensive knowledge of security, access, and social movement
  • Tactical awareness and counter-surveillance

Katherine played a critical operational role across the Session 9 combat and aftermath:

  • Shouted across the ballroom for the party to make for the windows, directing the escape route
  • Fired her pistol at the fleeing Adler, forcing him to the ground and halting his progress
  • Dove for and secured the command fork after Varrio burned it from Adler’s hand — experienced a brief, disorienting vision on first contact
  • Handed the fork to Georgiana without protest when asked in the withdrawing room
  • Dispatched Charles with the urgent note to Honoria
  • Administered first aid to Emma’s wound at the safehouse

At Thaliastraße 12. Functioning as the party’s operational foreman. Nell fled independently during the combat — Katherine’s newest asset exited without instructions, and where Nell is by dawn is an open question.

[!info] Player Agency Note Katherine is a player character. All descriptions refer to what is available to her or what she observes — never what she chooses to do or feel.
