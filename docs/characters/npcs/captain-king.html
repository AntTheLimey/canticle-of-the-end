Physical Description

Striking and charismatic, King presents as a dashing Naval officer with an easy confidence that commands attention in any drawing room. Their appearance and manner are carefully cultivated to support the fiction of a wealthy captain on shore leave.

Background

Born in 1777 as Wilhelmina King, daughter of a radical preacher. King leads the Air Loom Gang, a group of radicals seeking to make Britain more egalitarian. They masquerade as a wealthy Royal Navy captain using false service records created under the influence of the Air Loom, a mind-manipulating machine. King planned to use the Air Loom on high society at a soirée to advance the Gang’s revolutionary aims.
