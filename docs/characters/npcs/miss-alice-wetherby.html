Physical Description

Neatly kept, dark brown hair always pinned securely, sharp green eyes that miss nothing. Age 26. A perfectionist in her duties.

Background

Miss Alice Wetherby is Georgiana’s lady’s maid in Tarryford, Wiltshire. She comes from a long line of house staff — her mother was once lady’s maid to Georgiana’s mother. Quietly observant and fiercely loyal.

Skilled in dressmaking, discreetly intercepts unwanted letters, and knows how to handle a crisis with calm efficiency.
