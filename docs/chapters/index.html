The Beginning

Marina Garrick's solo adventures and the formation of the core party. Three investigations across the south of England, each one escalating from the uncanny to the lethal.

London

Complete

The Orphean Society investigation. Two missing Order agents, a vocal conditioning programme killing students, and a Stonehenge ritual to summon a servant of Yog-Sothoth.

Lyon

Complete

The Société Harmonique de l'Aube. Masquerade balls, tunnel expeditions beneath Lyon, a hospital raid, and a final assault on the Silkweavers' Guild. Four PCs died — the bloodiest chapter of the campaign.
