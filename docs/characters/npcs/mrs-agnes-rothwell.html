Background

Mrs. Agnes Rothwell is the housekeeper of Hartwell_House, the Order_of_St_Aelfric’s recovery and convalescent facility in London. A severe but competent woman in her late fifties, with sharp brown eyes and iron-grey hair pulled into a tight bun, she runs the house with an efficient and fair hand.

She speaks politely but firmly, and is unafraid to put even the highest-born residents in their place when necessary. The servants under her charge are discreet and well-trained.

Campaign History

  • Summer 1813: Received Marina_Garrick upon her arrival at Hartwell House with Lady_Honoria_Lyndhurst. Assessed Marina with a keen gaze and assigned Miss_Eleanor_Finch as her personal maid.
