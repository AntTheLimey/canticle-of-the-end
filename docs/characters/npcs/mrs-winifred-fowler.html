Physical Description

Round-faced, red-cheeked, always flour-dusted, with sharp blue eyes that see through every lie. Age 58.

Background

Mrs. Winifred “Winnie” Fowler is the Wentworth family cook in Tarryford, Wiltshire. Worked her way up from kitchen maid to head cook. Widowed young and never remarried. Gruff but deeply affectionate — treats the younger staff like her own children.

Makes the best pies in Hampshire, has an uncanny sense when food is being stolen, and uses gossip as currency.
