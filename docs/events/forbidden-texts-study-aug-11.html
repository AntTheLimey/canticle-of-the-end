Forbidden Texts Study Aug 11

Date August 11, 1814 Location Thaliastrasse_12_Safehouse
research
Outcome: Counter-ritual possibility identified; both researchers suffered mental breaks

Participants

  • Georgiana_Wentworth
  • Adrien_de_Montferrand

Summary

Georgiana and Adrien studied the Liber_Ivonis and De_Vermiis_Mysteriis at Thaliastraße 12, seeking intelligence on the machine beneath the university. Both suffered mental breaks. Together, their findings identified the possibility of a counter-ritual.

Context

The two tomes were recovered from the Polizeidirektion strong room in Session 10 via Fischbein’s legal intervention and Metternich’s writ. They had not been studied until Session 12.

Participants

  • Georgiana — studied the Liber_Ivonis. Discovered harmonic gate theory and the significance of 432 Hz as a resonant key tied to Yog-Sothoth’s domain. Suffered temporary madness (laughing and crying uncontrollably).
  • Adrien — studied the De_Vermiis_Mysteriis. Found counter-frequencies (tonal inversions that collapse harmonic fields). Suffered severe mental break: fainted, and his traumatic Pyrenees abbey memories projected visually onto the parlor window for the entire party to witness.

Outcome & Consequences

Discoveries:

  • Harmonic gate theory: specific sound frequencies can thin the barrier between dimensions
  • 432 Hz is a resonant key tied to Yog-Sothoth’s domain
  • Counter-frequencies exist: tonal inversions that collapse harmonic fields rather than sustain them
  • Cross-referencing the two texts could yield a counter-ritual using the tuning fork (which vibrates at exactly 432 Hz)

Sanity costs: Both researchers paid a significant price. Georgiana’s bout was temporary. Adrien’s trauma projection exposed his deepest secret to the entire party.

Forward impact: The counter-ritual was proposed at the War Council that evening. It represents the party’s only non-military option for disrupting the Engine.
