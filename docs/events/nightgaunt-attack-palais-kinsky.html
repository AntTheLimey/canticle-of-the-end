Nightgaunt Attack Palais Kinsky

Date August 7-8, 1814 (midnight to dawn) Location Palais_Kinsky, Vienna
battle
Outcome: Nightgaunt killed (Adrien delivers killing blow). Charlotte Thorne too severely wounded to continue — retires as Juel's PC. Mythos horror confirmed as physical reality for the entire party.

Participants

  • Nightgaunt — attacker
  • Emma_Wentworth
  • Georgiana_Wentworth
  • Adrien_de_Montferrand
  • Charlotte_Thorne — severely wounded — retires
  • Varrio_Harrowmont — witness; fainted at the sight
  • Thomas_Wyndham
  • Pemberton

Overview

Between midnight and dawn on the night of August 7–8, 1814. The Harmonic_Engine’s resonant frequency, having reached 87% operational purity, thinned the dimensional membrane separating Vienna from the Outer Spheres. A Nightgaunt — a creature of shadow and hunger that feeds on the terror of its victims — crossed over, drawn to the harmonic beacon emanating from the Sealed Anatomical Theatre. It hunted in Vienna’s streets and tracked the party to Palais_Kinsky.

Combat Summary

The Nightgaunt broke into the Palais Kinsky during the late hours. Combat occurred across multiple rooms on the upper floors. Key beats:

  • Georgiana_Wentworth identified the creature as a Nightgaunt from her reference tome (Revelations_of_Glaaki)
  • Georgiana_Wentworth drew swords in her nightgown — tactical combatant at close range
  • Emma_Wentworth bit the Nightgaunt’s neck, tearing away flesh — a campaign-defining moment of brutal courage
  • Charlotte_Thorne took a three-clawed slash across the shoulder. The wound was severe enough that she has been unable to continue field operations since
  • Varrio_Harrowmont fainted at the sight of the creature — PTSD response linked to witnessing Moreau’s death hours earlier
  • Adrien_de_Montferrand delivered the killing blow
  • Thomas_Wyndham subsequently maintained an armed vigil outside Emma’s door for the remainder of the night

Why This Matters

  • First Mythos entity encountered in Chapter 3. Previously, Vienna’s horror had been human — knives, institutional corruption, cult infiltration. The Nightgaunt proves the Engine is not merely a mechanical atrocity but an extradimensional gateway.
  • Charlotte Thorne’s retirement. The Nightgaunt’s wounds forced Juel’s original PC into permanent convalescence. She writes letters of retirement to Harcourt and Honoria on the morning of August 8 and becomes a Keeper NPC.
  • Juel transitions PCs. Katherine_Ward is dispatched by Harcourt to join the party on August 8.
  • Varrio’s PTSD is established. The fainting moment is a psychological vulnerability the campaign carries forward.
