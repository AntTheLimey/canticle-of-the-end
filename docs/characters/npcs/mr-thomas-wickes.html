Physical Description

Weather-worn but strong, with dark, wind-ruffled hair and calloused hands. Age 41.

Background

Thomas “Tom” Wickes is the Wentworth family coachman and stablemaster in Tarryford, Wiltshire. He grew up in the village, started as a stable boy, and worked his way up. Gruff and no-nonsense, but fiercely protective of Emma and Georgiana.

Expert horseman, a skilled bare-knuckle boxer in his youth, and knows every backroad in Hampshire.
