Physical Description

Tall, willowy, with pale skin and expressive eyes. A vision of Regency elegance — wears high fashion even offstage. Her beauty is matched by a glint of cunning behind the smiles. Carries a small silver mirror and watches her reflection obsessively since Giles vanished.

Background

Miss Eliza Sharpe plays Lady Teazle at Drury Lane. Once a merchant’s daughter from Brighton, she rose through London’s theatre scene on raw talent, fierce ambition, and whispered favour from an anonymous patron. Always performing, even in conversation — pretends not to know things she absolutely knows.
