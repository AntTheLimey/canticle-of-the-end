Adrien_de_Montferrand

Hosts Palais Kinsky, Order of St. Aelfric member, Delivered killing blow to Nightgaunt, Led charge to Polizeidirektion

Player Anna Points 200/200 Background French Aristocrat / Viscount Status alive
Vienna Chapter Status Benefit to the Investigators The Montferrand Collection — Occult Library and Field Archive The Resonance Compass — His Practical Invention Tactical Support Keeper Notes Campaign Design Notes Masquerade Ball — Session 9 Current Status (dawn, 9 August 1814) Connections to Other Files

During the Vienna chapter, Adrien proved himself through action:

  • Nightgaunt Combat (Session 6): Personally engaged and killed the creature attacking the party at his own residence (Palais_Kinsky)
  • Polizeidirektion Operation: Led the charge during the August 6 crisis
  • Order Recruitment: Sworn into service under Lord_Percival_Harcourt

He now hosts the party at his Palais Kinsky residence and serves as an active, trusted member of the investigation team.

Concrete Advantage: Adrien owns a country hunting lodge on the northern slopes above Lyon — a converted Roman watchtower that overlooks the Rhône valley. Recently, he discovered ancient symbols etched into its foundations, identical to those emerging in Mathilde_Savarin’s excavation.

He has also unearthed a sealed cistern chamber beneath the lodge containing:

  • Fragments of a broken harmonic lyre, clearly from the same Roman cult the Société Harmonique emulates.
  • A bronze key engraved with the same three-ring motif used by Mathilde_Savarin’s Blue Sash cultists.
  • A journal from a 2nd-century Roman engineer, translated poorly by Adrien, describing “the Voice Beneath the Earth” — a precursor myth to the Canticle.

His hunting lodge contains a private research wing, built around artifacts collected during his service and later travels. Within it, he keeps:

  • Transcribed Pyrenean monastic codices about “harmonic theophany” — precursors to the Canticle.
  • A weathered map of France annotated with acoustic ley-lines (drawn by an 18th-century Jesuit musicologist). Lyon sits at one of the “Resonant Nodes.”
  • Several volumes from the Abbey of St-Jean-Pied-de-Port, whose monks studied “voices in stone.”
  • A locked cabinet containing sketches of geometrical resonators and a fragment of Roman bronze marked with the same interlocked-rings motif found on Mathilde_Savarin’s cult insignia.

Adrien has constructed a small brass “harmonic compass”, an experimental device he designed to detect sub-audible vibrations in stone (originally for geology).

He demonstrates it by placing it near a tuning fork—its needle oscillates toward sources of resonant frequency. He confides that in Lyon’s Roman quarter the needle moves on its own, even in silence.

Game Effect:

  • Acts as a tracking device for the Société Harmonique’s rehearsals or rituals:
    • Within 200 yards of an active harmonic source, it begins to tremble audibly.
    • At the abandoned guild hall, the oscillations grow violent—confirming the cult’s location.
  • Provides a tangible, mechanical way to locate the cult’s hidden rehearsal site.
  • Can later react to the ritual itself—serving as a dramatic tension gauge (the needle thrashing wildly as the Canticle begins).
  • As a Bourbon noble with local estate ties, Adrien can secure legal access papers or nighttime permissions (hunting licenses, carriage passes) giving the party plausible reason to move through restricted districts after curfew.
  • His estate guards and stablehands can act as temporary NPC allies or distractions during operations.
  • During confrontations, he has personally contributed armed combat and tactical initiative.
  • Emotional Tone: Adrien is a character shaped by trauma and duty — capable, wounded, and committed to preventing others’ suffering.
  • Romantic Dynamic: His connection to Marina_Garrick (if pursued by Anna) should be intense but quiet — two people drawn together not by flirtation but recognition of shared scars.
  • Plot Function: His estate, research, and personal agency give the investigators direct evidence of the cult’s ancient roots — bypassing purely social leads.
  • PC Agency: As a player character, Adrien’s actions, emotions, and choices are entirely driven by Anna’s decisions. Do not prescribe his reactions or feelings.

Adrien represents the intersection of past trauma and present purpose — a man driven by the need to prevent others from experiencing what he has endured. His arc is one of finding meaning through action and connection.

Adrien fashioned makeshift weapons from jagged shards of coloured glass attached to strips of lead from the shattered ballroom window. He threw one at a Wächter but missed — the improvised weapon spinning dangerously close to Varrio’s face. He fell from a window during the escape but was caught and cushioned by Varrio.

Carrying forward: Vogel personally witnessed Adrien’s civilian shooting from Session 8 and fled the ballroom after identifying him. Vogel’s legal pretext for arrest is untouched. Caroline is now named by Adler as a future Brotherhood target — Adrien’s open attachment to her doubles as operational danger.

At Thaliastraße 12. Physically uninjured. The civilian shooting charge from Session 8 remains an active legal threat via Vogel.
