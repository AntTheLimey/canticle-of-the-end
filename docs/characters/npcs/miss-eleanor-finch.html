Physical Description

Soft-spoken but observant, with a quiet intelligence behind her blue-grey eyes. Curtsies without lowering her gaze entirely — a balance of deference and confidence that suggests she is used to managing affairs with discretion.

Background

Eleanor Finch was born into service, the daughter of a well-respected governess and a footman with military history. She grew up in the household of a noble family, privy to whispered secrets and the quiet power dynamics of the gentry.

Her mother’s sudden death left her adrift, forcing her into domestic service at a young age. Her keen eye and sharp instincts caught the attention of Lady_Honoria_Lyndhurst, who saw potential beyond simple household duties. Honoria trains her in self-defence, firearms, and information gathering — a complex relationship in which Honoria sees Eleanor as useful but does not coddle her.

Eleanor now serves as both Marina’s maid and a quiet informant for the Order_of_St_Aelfric, using her ability to blend into conversations unnoticed to gather valuable information.

Samuel Finch (Younger Brother)

Once her closest companion, Samuel was sent to a workhouse when their mother died. By the time Eleanor had the means to search for him, he had vanished into the underbelly of London. She occasionally receives whispers of his name among street informants, but the truth remains unknown. She carries a locket containing a faded sketch of him.

Roleplaying Eleanor

Quietly intelligent — notices everything, rarely speaks unless necessary. Quick-witted and adaptable, she navigates both high society and the servant quarters, shifting her demeanour as needed. Loyal but cautious — she respects Marina and Honoria but does not trust easily. A survivor’s mentality: she knows the powerful play their games, and it is best to stay ahead of them.

Adept at extracting secrets from servants, gossiping ladies, and drunk gentlemen alike. If trouble arises, Eleanor is often gone before anyone realises she was ever there.
