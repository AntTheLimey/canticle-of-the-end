Background

Mr. Tobias Whitmore is a senior member of the Order_of_St_Aelfric, a former university professor now dedicated to the Order’s research. He resides at Hartwell_House, where he occupies a well-stocked study on the second floor.

Tall, lean, with graying hair and an ink-stained waistcoat, his presence is more academic than aristocratic. He speaks in measured tones, though his enthusiasm for hidden knowledge is evident.

Campaign History

  • Summer 1813: Met Marina_Garrick at Hartwell House during her induction into the Order. Engaged her with questions testing her intellectual curiosity: “Do you believe, Miss Garrick, that knowledge is a weapon — or a curse?”
  • Winter 1813 (Training Montage): One of six instructors who trained the investigators at Hartwell House between The Long Corridor and The Scandal Beneath the Stage. Fellow instructors: Miss_Beatrice_Harrow, Mr_Alfred_Smythe, Miss_Dorothea_Bexley, Mr_Benedict_Fairfax, Lady_Wilhelmina_Danvers.

Character Notes

  • Potential mentor figure for investigators with scholarly interests
  • His study is filled with open books and meticulous notes on occult subjects
  • Could serve as a research resource if the party returns to England
