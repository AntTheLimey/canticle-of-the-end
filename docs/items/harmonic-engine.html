Harmonic Engine

ritual_instrument

Current Holder: Albin_Herzfeld (located in the Sealed_Anatomical_Theatre beneath the University_of_Vienna)

Origin: Brotherhood of the Open Measure; designed by Professor Albin Herzfeld

Description

A 30-foot biomechanical instrument constructed from human body parts, installed in the Sealed_Anatomical_Theatre beneath the University_of_Vienna. The Engine is Herzfeld’s contribution to the Grand Canticle — Segment V, the Vienna cell’s ritual offering to Yog-Sothoth.

Components:

  • Pipes: Bronzed esophageal tubes serve as wind pipes
  • Bellows: Human lungs as pneumatic chambers
  • Keys: Arms and hands wired to mechanical levers
  • Heart: A living human brain at the Engine’s centre, interpreting the Canticle score
  • Victims: Fully conscious but paralysed via Austrian alchemical neurotoxins

The Engine sacrifices multiple humans simultaneously while keeping them aware of their fate — a deliberate amplification of suffering that feeds the harmonic resonance required for the ritual.

Properties

  • Operating frequency: A=432 Hz — the resonant key tied to the domain of Yog-Sothoth
  • Sub-audible vibration: The Engine produces a physical pressure below the threshold of hearing, felt as a rhythmic thrumming in the chest. Katherine and Nell felt it from the university rooftop during their Session 12 reconnaissance.
  • Sympathetic resonance: The Command_Tuning_Fork vibrates in sympathy with the Engine even at a distance. Georgiana felt her chest resonate when Katherine described the sensation.
  • Counter-vulnerability: The De_Vermiis_Mysteriis describes counter-frequencies — tonal inversions that could collapse harmonic fields. Georgiana and Adrien identified this in Session 12 and believe a counter-ritual is possible.

Completion Status (11 August 1814)

Approximately 85% complete as of August 3. Main frame, lower register pipes, bellows, keys, preservation fluid circulation, and acoustic chamber modifications are all functional.

Outstanding requirements:

  1. Soprano voice (CRITICAL — DISRUPTED): Anna was the irreplaceable target. She is in party custody. Caroline_Hartley was named as a reserve target but the Brotherhood has not yet moved on her.
  2. Conductor brain (CRITICAL): The current brain (a philosopher captured six months ago) is deteriorating. Herzfeld has considered using himself.
  3. Final calibration (3 nights): Requires the Engine partially active with all components. Cannot begin until the soprano is installed.
  4. Ritual score (COMPLETE): Herzfeld has the full notation encoded in a harmonic-interval cipher (two copies — office and private apartments).

By-products

The Harmonische_Wachter are failed integration subjects — human bodies that rejected the Engine’s modifications. Rather than discard them, the Brotherhood repurposed them as guard creatures, controlled via the command fork’s A=432 Hz pitch.
