Aelfric Brooch

relic

Current Holder: Each female PC receives one upon initiation

Origin: Order_of_St_Aelfric

Physical Description

An ornate, circular brooch of antique silver, featuring a thorn-wrapped key flanked by wings, radiating lines of light like a divine sunburst. At its heart lies a five-pointed star, and below, a scroll bears the motto: “Lux in Tenebris.”

Pinned to the chest or high shoulder of a riding cloak, pelisse, or gown. Though eye-catching, it passes for a fashionable antiquarian piece.

Properties

  • Symbol of Initiation — Denotes trusted membership in the Order_of_St_Aelfric. Recognised by other Order members and operatives as proof of standing.
  • Memory Anchor — Prevents time-loop-induced amnesia; the wearer retains knowledge of previous iterations.
  • Subtle Glow — Faintly shimmers when exposed to powerful arcane forces, particularly those tied to the Aeternum_Choir.

Distribution

Each female PC receives an Ælfric Brooch upon initiation into the Order. The male equivalent is the Aelfric_Cravat_Pin.
