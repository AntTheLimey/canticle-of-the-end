Overview

Sergeant Franz Huber is a street-level police operative corrupted by Baron von Kaunitz’s payments. He is not a true believer in the Aeternum Choir — he simply takes money from Kaunitz to report on “persons of interest.” He doesn’t know about the cult, just that a wealthy baron pays well for information.

Quick Facts

  • Role: Street surveillance, information gathering, reports on investigators
  • Motivation: Pure mercenary (money)
  • Loyalty: None; will switch sides for better payment or threat
  • Information: Can identify investigators being watched, delay reports, lose evidence
