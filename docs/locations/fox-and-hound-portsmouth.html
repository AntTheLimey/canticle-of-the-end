Overview

A coaching inn in Portsmouth where Marina_Garrick arrived wounded and suffering from temporary madness after fleeing Osney_Grange in the curate’s stolen coach, Summer 1813. The inn served as Marina’s recovery point before Lady_Honoria_Lyndhurst arrived to recruit her for the Order_of_St_Aelfric.

Key NPCs

  • Mrs_Margaret_Fairchild — Innkeeper’s wife. Cared for Marina upon her arrival.
  • Dr_Ambrose_Hargreaves — Naval surgeon lodging at the inn. Treated Marina’s gunshot wound.

Campaign Significance

The Fox & Hound is where Marina’s trajectory changed — from traumatised fugitive to Order recruit. Lady_Honoria_Lyndhurst found her here and gave her the ultimatum: “Come to London and learn the truth, or walk away forever.”

What Happened Here

Summer 1813 Marina Recruitment Portsmouth
Marina accepted Lady Honoria's offer and was recruited into the Order of St. Aelfric. She travelled to London, was inducted at Ravenwood House by Lord Percival Harcourt, and took up residence at Hartwell House.
