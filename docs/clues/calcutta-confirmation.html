Calcutta Confirmation

documentary high (intercepted correspondence) Party (via intelligence chain)

Description

A letter from Acharya_Devendra_Ghosh addressed to Herzfeld, recovered from his correspondence in Session 12. The letter confirms Calcutta as an active ritual city and references “the Hugli bends inland” — a geographical marker pointing to the ritual site. This is the primary campaign-bridge clue connecting Vienna (Chapter 3) to Calcutta (Chapter 4).

Three Clue Rule Assessment

Currently a single-source clue. For Chapter 4 prep, backup delivery paths should be established:

  • Herzfeld’s office documents (if the party searches during the University assault)
  • Der Kantor’s correspondence (if Munich coordination intelligence is captured)
  • Harcourt’s Order intelligence network (independent confirmation)

Status

Delivered in Session 12. The party has the letter. Chapter 4 prep should ensure at least two additional independent paths to the same conclusion.
