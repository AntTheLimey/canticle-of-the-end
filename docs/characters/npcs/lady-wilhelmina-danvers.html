Background

Lady Wilhelmina “Mina” Danvers is a widow of impeccable standing, long embedded within London’s highest circles. Known among the Order_of_St_Aelfric as “The Silver Duchess” — though she bears no such title. Regal, statuesque, with silver-streaked dark hair and perfectly tailored gowns in deep hues. She views social graces not as frippery, but as survival skills.

A former lady-in-waiting to a royal cousin, she has personally negotiated safe passage for Order operatives through dangerous salons, occult gatherings, and duels of words and wit.

Poised, sharp-tongued, effortlessly commanding.

Training Skills

Charm, Credit Rating, Persuade (three skills — added at the players’ request for a society instructor)

Campaign History

  • Winter 1813 (Training Montage): The sixth and final instructor added to the Hartwell House training programme at the players’ request. Taught the investigators to navigate ballrooms and drawing rooms. Her test: a simulated ball — greet correctly, dance the figures, hold conversation without a social faux pas.
