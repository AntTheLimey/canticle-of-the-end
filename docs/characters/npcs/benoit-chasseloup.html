Overview

Benoît Chasseloup is a former member of the Silkweavers’ Guild who served as a contact for the Société_Harmonique_de_l_Aube, providing access to the tunnel network beneath Lyon. His current status is unknown — he is presumed missing or dead. His tunnel maps fell into the hands of the investigators, though whether this was accidental or a deliberate trap remains unclear.

Campaign History

Chapter 2 — Lyon

Chasseloup’s tunnel maps proved instrumental to the investigators’ operations in Lyon. His disappearance raises questions about whether Savarin eliminated him after his usefulness ended, or whether he fled. His possible coercion by Mathilde_Savarin suggests he was not a willing participant in the cult’s activities.
