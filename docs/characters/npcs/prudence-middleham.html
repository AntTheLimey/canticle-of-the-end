Overview

Mrs. Prudence Middleham is a 57-year-old widow who rents rooms to genteel young ladies in Bloomsbury. She prides herself on discretion, cleanliness, and propriety, and considers herself a motherly figure to her lodgers, whether they appreciate it or not.

She was the last known person to speak with Imogen Bellamy before her disappearance.

Description

Round of face and firmly corseted, Middleham wears black bombazine and a frilled cap. Her spectacles hang from a ribbon. She always carries a ring of keys and a lavender-scented handkerchief. She moves with the determination of one who has kept a house since age fourteen.

Personality & Traits

  • Gossipy but cautious; protective of her reputation
  • Deeply believes in moral order and dangers of “musical men”
  • Distrustful of students who don’t eat regularly
  • Suspicious of Italians and foreigners
