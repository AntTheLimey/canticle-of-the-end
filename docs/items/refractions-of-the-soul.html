Campaign Relevance

The title suggests a text concerned with perception, optics, and the relationship between light and the soul — themes that align closely with Georgiana’s scholarly interests and the campaign’s treatment of the fork’s sensory effects (colour distortion, alien spectra, the visual phenomena accompanying harmonic resonance).

  • The “Refractions” may describe how light behaves at dimensional boundaries — the visual equivalent of the harmonic binding described in the Liber_Ivonis
  • “On Lights & Loss” may refer to what is lost when perception expands beyond human norms — a thematic mirror to Georgiana’s escalating fork bond
  • The text may contain practical information about the visual phenomena associated with Mythos contact, which would make it a field reference for recognising dimensional intrusion through optical anomalies
