Overview

Northlake Hall is the estate of the Northlake family in Tarryford, Wiltshire. It was the site of the autumn ball and the events of The Long Corridor adventure (Regency Cthulhu, Chaosium) in Autumn 1813 — the moment the campaign expanded from solo play to a full table.

The Northlake Family

  • Sir James Northlake — Head of the family
  • Lady Lydia Northlake — His wife, influential host and matchmaker
  • Miss Elizabeth Northlake — Their daughter, quietly observant, subject of scrutiny and rumour
  • Emma — The Dowager Lady Northlake

The Long Corridor

The estate contains a portal to a dark extradimensional realm inhabited by Horrors — squat, headless, fur-covered humanoids with torso-mounted eyes and fanged mouths. The portal took days to fully open.

Campaign History

The investigators entered twice. The first expedition got out before the Horrors reached them, but one creature escaped overnight and killed sheep on the estate. The party warned the Northlake family, who prepared to flee with their daughter Elizabeth.

On the second expedition, NPC men and servants went in armed alongside the investigators. Georgiana_Wentworth performed a blood ritual at an altar in the Dark Realm, closing the portal. The Horrors then descended. The armed men stopped to shoot, covering the retreat — they were killed. James_Bennet was overtaken while running, dismembered and consumed — the first PC death of the campaign. His death slowed the creatures enough for the surviving investigators to escape.

The Northlake Ball

The annual autumn ball at Northlake Hall brought together the eligible gentry of Tarryford and the surrounding region. See Tarryford#The Northlake Ball — Social Dynamics for the full pairing dynamics.

Campaign Significance

Northlake Hall is where the core party formed. Marina_Garrick was sent here by Lord_Percival_Harcourt on her first Order mission. The estate and its family remain available if the party returns to England.
