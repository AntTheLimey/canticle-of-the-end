Georgiana_Wentworth

Sharp-minded, Tactical combatant, Phobias of theatre and fish, Identified Nightgaunt from Revelations of Gla'aki

Player Beth Points 200/200 Background Gentlewoman (Wiltshire) Status alive
Background Current Status (Vienna, August 1814) Key Moments Connections & Threads Phobias Current Status (dawn, 9 August 1814)

Georgiana Wentworth is Emma’s sister and has been part of the campaign since London (Chapter 1). She is sharp-minded and carries lasting phobias from the investigations: fear of theatre (from the Drury Lane incident) and fear of fish (from The Long Corridor).

Georgiana is combat-capable and tactically sound. During the Nightgaunt attack at Palais Kinsky (Session 6), she drew swords in her nightgown and fought effectively. Crucially, she identified the creature as a Nightgaunt using the Revelations of Gla’aki — an identification that gave the party context for what they faced.

  • London Through Vienna: Original investigator, no character changes
  • Nightgaunt Combat (Session 6): Drew swords mid-attack, fought tactically
  • Creature Identification: Recognized and identified the Nightgaunt using occult knowledge
  • Masquerade Ball Session 8: Identified Adler publicly to Nikolai, triggering the Russian pursuit; extracted Anna from Adler’s control
  • Masquerade Ball Session 9 — Defining combatant: Spotted Baron von Kaunitz slipping away via the balcony and identified Captain Vogel beneath his domino mask. Raked a Wächter with her sword in the corridor, spilling dark ichor. Hamstrung Adler by slicing both Achilles tendons in a single sword stroke, ending his escape. Briefly established a psychic link with a Wächter through the command fork, seeing through its alien senses and forcing it to sit submissively — the link cost 1 SAN. Took possession of the fork from Katherine in the withdrawing room; it has not left her person since.
  • Sister Bond: Emma is her sister; they travel together
  • The Party: Full investigator, no formal Order affiliation stated
  • Occult Knowledge: Demonstrated familiarity with Mythos references
  • Command_Tuning_Fork: In Georgiana’s possession since the withdrawing room at Palais_Lobkowitz, dawn 9 August 1814. The fork looked back at her — a subplot seed.
  • Nikolai_Volkonsky: Bond deepened from waltz partner to comrades who survived the same night
  • Theatre (from Drury Lane)
  • Fish (from The Long Corridor)

At Thaliastraße 12 with the command fork. Lost 1 SAN to the Wächter-sight trespass. Otherwise physically uninjured. The closest of the party to Nikolai as a grieving comrade.

[!info] Player Agency Note Georgiana is a player character. All descriptions refer to what is available to her or what she observes — never what she chooses to do or feel.
