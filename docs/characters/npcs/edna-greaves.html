Overview

Edna Greaves is the 27-year-old niece and clerk of Tobias Greaves. Sharp-eyed and suspicious, she keeps the pawnshop accounts and distrusts most patrons, especially those who seem “artsy” or out of place.

Description

Thin-faced and watchful, Edna dresses neatly but plainly. She moves with efficient purpose and rarely smiles.

Personality & Traits

  • Distrustful and protective of her uncle’s interests
  • Sharp with accounts and details
  • Skeptical of strangers
  • May intervene if she believes investigators are trying to steal or deceive

Roleplaying Hooks

  • Harder to charm than her uncle; requires respect and directness
  • Will defend family interests fiercely
  • Can be won over with honesty and demonstrated concern for Clara’s welfare

Relationship to Campaign

Edna is a very minor NPC who serves as a potential obstacle or ally depending on investigator approach. She may verify or refute information about Clara’s visit to the pawnshop.
