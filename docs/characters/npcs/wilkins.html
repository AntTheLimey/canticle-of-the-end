Overview

Wilkins is a factory foreman at Grosvenor Works. He maintains order among the workers and oversees operations. He may have observed or participated in cult-related activities involving the factory.

Key Information

  • May have been involved in suspicious activities
  • Potential source of information about unusual workers or visitors

Roleplaying Hooks

  • Protective of his authority and the factory’s reputation
  • Responds to authority and official inquiries
  • May be bribed or intimidated, depending on approach

Relationship to Campaign

Wilkins serves as a gatekeeper to factory-based investigation and provides potential access or obstacles depending on investigator approach.
