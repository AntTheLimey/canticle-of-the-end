Aelfric Cravat Pin

relic

Current Holder: Each male PC receives one upon initiation

Origin: Order_of_St_Aelfric

Physical Description

A refined silver stickpin, designed as a winged key entwined with thorns. At its centre, a jet-black stone etched with a luminescent pentagram glows faintly in the dark. Compact, mysterious, and suitable for a neckcloth or waistcoat.

Tucked into a gentleman’s cravat, or fastened to the inner lining of a waistcoat. Designed for discretion and elegance.

Properties

  • Symbol of Brotherhood — Confirms initiation and trust within the Order_of_St_Aelfric. Recognised by other Order members as proof of standing.
  • Arcane Safeguard — Protects against temporal disturbances and memory loss during magical phenomena.
  • Wards Against Madness — Grants +1 bonus on rolls to resist sanity loss from time-related Mythos events (Keeper’s discretion).

Distribution

Each male PC receives an Ælfric Pin upon initiation into the Order. The female equivalent is the Aelfric_Brooch.

Relationships

  • Symbol of Order of St Aelfric — Official insignia of trusted Order membership; male counterpart to the Ælfric Brooch
  • Counterpart of Aelfric Brooch — Male equivalent of the female Ælfric Brooch; same protective properties
  • Resonates with Aeternum Choir — Jet-black stone glows faintly when exposed to powerful arcane forces
