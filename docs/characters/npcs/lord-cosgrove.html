Physical Description

Red-faced and gouty, Lord Cosgrove was once a handsome man, but years of excess have taken their toll. At forty-three he is brash, loud, and extravagant in both dress and manner.

Background

The penniless Earl of Cosgrove was notorious in Brighton society for his gambling debts and bluster. Despite his empty coffers, he maintained an extravagant lifestyle and a loud presence at every social function. He attempted to blackmail Captain_King and was killed by the Air Loom prototype on September 18, 1812, in the Pavilion Gardens.
