Collarbone_Jack Stub

Role Coffeehouse Regular Nationality British Status alive Age unknown

Overview

“Collarbone” Jack is a regular at The Laughing Fox known for gossiping and trading information for drinks. He has witnessed Clara’s peculiar behavior during her performances.

Key Information

  • Claims Clara once sang a song that made someone pass out
  • Will trade information for a drink or coin
  • May have useful gossip about the Society or its members if properly incentivized

Roleplaying Hooks

  • Easily bribed with alcohol
  • Gossips freely but unreliably; information should be verified
  • May exaggerate or fabricate details for entertainment

Relationship to Campaign

Jack provides atmospheric color and potential (if unreliable) information about Clara’s performances and strange incidents at the coffeehouse.
