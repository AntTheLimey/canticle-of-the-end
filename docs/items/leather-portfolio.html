Leather Portfolio

intelligence asset

Current Holder: Investigators (party)

Origin: Hidden compartment in Thaliastrasse_12_Safehouse writing desk

Description

A sealed leather case found behind a false panel in a writing desk on the third floor of Thaliastraße 12. Discovered by Varrio and Georgiana in Session 12 after Georgiana’s spell confirmed the compartment was untrapped.

Properties

Contains:

  • Forged identity papers
  • Letters of introduction on British Embassy letterhead bearing what appears to be Lord Harcourt’s signature
  • A coded cipher key
  • 200 florins in small notes

History

Pre-positioned emergency escape kit. The owner is unknown. The British Embassy letterhead and apparent Harcourt signature suggest an Order connection, but the portfolio’s presence was not mentioned by Thurner (the safehouse’s owner) or by Harcourt. The 200 florins contributed to the party’s available funds for the Ferrante mercenary contract.
