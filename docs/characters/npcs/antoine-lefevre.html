Overview

Antoine Lefèvre is a civil clerk in Lyon’s city offices, coerced into serving the Société_Harmonique_de_l_Aube through threats to his family. He handles permits, misdirects officials, and sabotages gentry investigations on behalf of the cult. His motivation is fear and blackmail rather than belief.

[!note] Distinct Character Antoine Lefèvre is a different person from Victoire_Lefevre — they are from different families.

Campaign History

Chapter 2 — Lyon

Antoine was heard plotting with Luc_Fouchard by Emma and Charlotte during the Lyon chapter. The nature of their conversation and the circumstances of the encounter established him as a cult functionary operating under duress.
