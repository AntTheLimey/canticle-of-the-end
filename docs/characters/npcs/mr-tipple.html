Overview

Mr. Tipple is the jaded owner of The Laughing Fox, a bohemian coffeehouse in Soho frequented by journalists, musicians, and working-class patrons. A former musician himself, he has developed a sympathetic eye for struggling performers.

Description

Weathered and weary, Tipple moves behind the bar with practiced efficiency.

Personality & Traits

  • Jaded but compassionate; has seen enough hardship to be sympathetic
  • Unflappable; doesn’t ask questions
  • Will provide information about Clara if approached respectfully

Key Information

  • Knows Clara as “Anna” and has sheltered her
  • Can describe her mental state and behavior
  • Will testify to her fear of music and strange reactions

Roleplaying Hooks

  • Will speak to investigators about Clara if they seem genuinely concerned for her welfare
  • Can point them toward other regulars who may have witnessed unusual behavior

Relationship to Campaign

Tipple is a minor NPC who provides context for Clara’s current situation and serves as a potential source of information about the coffeehouse and its patrons.
