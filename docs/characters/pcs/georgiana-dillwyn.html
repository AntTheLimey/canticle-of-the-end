Lady Georgiana Dillwyn was a fellow investigator and friend of Marina_Garrick during the Loom & Lucidity adventure in Brighton, Spring 1813. She was played by Elizabeth (Ant’s daughter) during the early solo sessions.

Little is known about her background beyond her participation in the Brighton investigation. She accompanied Marina through the social events of Brighton society, including the soirée at Captain King’s drawing room.

  • Loom & Lucidity (Spring 1813): Investigated alongside Marina Garrick in Brighton. Attended social events with the Hamptons and interacted with Brighton society figures including Captain King, Lord Ratcliffe, and Mrs. Pemberton.

UNKNOWN. Georgiana Dillwyn’s fate after the Brighton adventure is unrecorded. She did not accompany Marina to Osney_Grange or Portsmouth. Whether she returned to her family, remained in Brighton, or was contacted by the Order is undetermined.
