Overview

Leopoldstadt is Vienna’s Jewish quarter, located across the Danube Canal to the east of the city center. It is a marginal district of narrow streets, underworld contacts, cheap lodgings, and underground networks. Dr. Brenner has hidden here, and it serves as a location for meeting with marginal contacts.

Key Locations Within Leopoldstadt

  • Der Schiefe Schornstein (The Crooked Chimney): Disreputable tavern where Brenner drinks; known to street people
  • Widow Katz’s Boarding House: Yellow door; where Brenner was found; Widow Katz is sharp-eyed informant
  • Jewish Cemetery: Where the Bauer brothers may be obtaining bodies
  • Underground Networks: Fences, black market contacts, hidden safe houses
