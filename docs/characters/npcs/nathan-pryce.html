Overview

Nathan Pryce is a furtive, watchful cultist who serves as a guard in the sub-basement detention area. He is loyal to Lady Danforth and positions himself near the vault containing ritual materials. Nervous and paranoid, he is vigilant but potentially exploitable through careful psychological manipulation.

Description

Thin and wary, Pryce moves with the controlled precision of someone always prepared for violence.

Personality & Traits

  • Watchful and paranoid; suspicious of strangers
  • Believes strongly in Danforth’s vision for the Canticle
  • Nervous energy; easily startled
  • Prone to overreacting to perceived threats
