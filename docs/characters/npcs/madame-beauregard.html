Background

Madame Beauregard is a sharp-witted, stylish Frenchwoman who owns Brighton’s most fashionable modiste’s shop. Dressing the town’s elite, she occupies a position of peculiar social power — privy to the confidences and measurements of half the gentry in Sussex. She was encountered during the Loom & Lucidity investigation in spring 1813.

Campaign Significance

Minor NPC from Brighton during the Loom & Lucidity investigation. Her shop is an ideal node for social intelligence — if the party returns to England, Madame Beauregard is one of the best-placed sources of local gossip available to a female investigator.
