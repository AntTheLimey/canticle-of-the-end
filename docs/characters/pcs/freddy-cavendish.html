Freddy Cavendish

Socially connected, Produced Masquerade invitations, Witnessed Nightgaunt attack

Player Jay Age 22 Nationality British Background Gentleman (Second Son) Status alive
Background Attributes Skills Key Relationships Key Moments Current Status

The Honourable Frederick Cavendish, second son of an English noble family. Twenty-two years old, fashionable, and possessed of the easy confidence that comes with a good name and better connections. Freddy arrived in Vienna as a gentleman of leisure — attending balls, collecting invitations, and making himself agreeable to the right people.

He is not a member of the Order_of_St_Aelfric and carries no formal intelligence training. His entanglement with the investigators came through social proximity and bad timing. He has since witnessed enough — the Nightgaunt at Palais_Kinsky, the Wächter at the Masquerade — to know that dangerous, inexplicable forces are at work in Vienna.

Character statistics not yet recorded.

  • Etiquette and Society: Freddy moves through Viennese high society with ease, producing invitations and introductions that the other investigators cannot
  • Improvised Combat: Built a torch from cloth and a chair leg during the Masquerade assault, adopting Varrio’s fire doctrine under pressure
  • Pemberton (valet): Active field support, carries equipment and assists with operations
  • Adrien_de_Montferrand: Hosted at Adrien’s Palais_Kinsky residence
  • The investigators: Through his connections, Freddy provides social access the party cannot easily gain elsewhere
  • Masquerade Invitations: Personally secured invitations and masks for the party’s attendance at the Grand Masquerade
  • Nightgaunt Attack (Session 6): Witnessed the creature’s assault on Palais_Kinsky; partially briefed on the supernatural threat
  • Masquerade Ball (Session 9): Held his nerve beside a bleeding Russian officer (Vladimir) during the Wächter incursion. Built an improvised torch and fought alongside the party. Attempted to pull the wounded Vladimir to safety

At Thaliastraße 12, dawn of 9 August 1814. Physically uninjured.

Relationships

  • Friend of Adrien de Montferrand — Fellow Viennese socialite and party attendee
  • Knows Emma Wentworth — Party socialite
  • Employs Pemberton — Freddy's valet, field support operative
  • Knows Katherine Ward — Order operative, recent party member
