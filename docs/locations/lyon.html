Description

A major French city and setting for Chapter 2 of the campaign. The investigators travel from London to Lyon to disrupt the Société Harmonique, the Aeternum Choir’s Lyon cell. The silk trade, Bourbon restoration politics, and the Blue_Sashes resistance movement form the chapter’s backdrop.

Notable Features

  • Historic silk-weaving quarter (Croix-Rousse)
  • The confluence of the Rhône and Saône rivers
  • Site of the second Aeternum Choir cell disruption
