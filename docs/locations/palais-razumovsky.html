Description

The palatial residence of the Russian diplomatic delegation in Vienna, located in the Landstraße district. Originally built for Count Razumovsky, the palace now serves as the Russian embassy and military intelligence headquarters. Major Volkonsky maintains his office here.

Campaign Appearances

  • Session 12: Georgiana visited Palais Razumovsky to meet Major Volkonsky and negotiate Russian military support for the University assault. The meeting succeeded — Andrei committed five Russian soldiers including himself to the operation.
