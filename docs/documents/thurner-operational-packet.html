Thurner Operational Packet

intelligence_brief

Document Overview

A folded paper packet, approximately four pages of closely-written instructions in a small clear hand, delivered to Georgiana_Wentworth by Major William Thurner via a waltz at the Grand Masquerade on the evening of 8 August 1814. Thurner prepared the packet over the course of that day, drawing on Austrian Order intelligence and his own field assessment.

Content Summary

Four key operational items, each briefly laid out:

  1. Herzfeld is alone tonight. Albin_Herzfeld is in residence at the Sealed Anatomical Theatre basement, accompanied by at most two armed guards and the unconscious or paralysed victims awaiting Engine integration. No other senior Brotherhood operatives are at the University during the Masquerade.
  2. Reconnaissance now, operations later. Thurner’s professional judgement: a rushed raid on the University tonight will fail. The party needs to gather intelligence, verify approaches, and act deliberately. “Seven days remain.” (Stated from Thurner’s perspective; the actual ritual deadline is August 15 midnight, six nights plus the daylight of the 9th.)
  3. Café Frauenhuber — Order emergency contact. On Himmelpfortgasse. Protocol: sit at the table nearest the window, order Turkish coffee, leave it untouched. A contact arrives within the hour. Single-use emergency channel.
  4. Thaliastraße 12 — Order safehouse. Josefstadt district. Contains: two cavalry pistols, forty rounds of ammunition, a sabre, rope, three lanterns. Do not visit unless desperate. Being followed there burns the asset.

Why This Matters

  • The packet is the first operational asset list the party has received in Vienna. Before this, they had Fischbein and the White Ox, but no Order-specific caches.
  • Both named assets are one-shot — using them spends the Order’s Vienna infrastructure. The party should deploy them carefully.
  • Herzfeld’s isolation tonight is a time-bound opportunity. Even if the party cannot act tonight (they are committed to the Masquerade), the intelligence defines the shape of Session 9 planning.
