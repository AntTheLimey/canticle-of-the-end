Grand Masquerade Palais Lobkowitz

Evening of August 8 into early hours of August 9, 1814 — Palais Lobkowitz, Minoritenplatz, Vienna
Anna rescued. Adler exposed publicly and fleeing. Mikhail dead. Catastrophic physical damage to Palais Lobkowitz. Brotherhood's secrecy broken in a single night. Duel scheduled for dawn August 9. Session ends mid-Round 3 of combat.
