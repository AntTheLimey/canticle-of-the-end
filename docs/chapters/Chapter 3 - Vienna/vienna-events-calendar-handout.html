Vienna Events Calendar Handout

August 1814

As published in the Wiener Zeitung

AUGUST 4

8 PM — Mozart’s Don Giovanni Burgtheater | Tickets available

AUGUST 5

4 PM — Salon Concert Residence of Countess von Thun | By invitation

8 PM — Chamber Music Evening Palais Lobkowitz | Haydn & Beethoven string quartets

AUGUST 6

7 PM — Imperial Reception Hofburg Palace | Diplomatic guests

9 PM — Public Concert Augarten Pavilion | Military band, open to all

AUGUST 7

3 PM — Piano Recital Vienna Conservatory | Student performances, open to public

8 PM — Italian Opera Excerpts Theater an der Wien | Visiting company from Milan

AUGUST 8

9 PM — GRAND MASQUERADE BALL Palais Lobkowitz | Costumes required, by invitation

AUGUST 9

4 PM — Lieder Recital Palais Esterházy | Songs by Herr Schubert

8 PM — Beethoven Symphony Burgtheater | The master himself conducting

AUGUST 10

8 PM — VOCAL CONCERT Burgtheater | Featuring Fräulein Anna Lindqvist, Swedish soprano “The voice of an angel”

AUGUST 11

3 PM — University Lecture University of Vienna | Prof. Herzfeld: “Acoustical Mathematics”

8 PM — Comic Opera Theater an der Wien

AUGUST 12

8 PM — GRAND BALL Schönbrunn Palace | Imperial invitation only

AUGUST 13

7 PM — Sacred Music Concert St. Stephen’s Cathedral | Choral works, free admission

AUGUST 14

All Day — Feast of the Assumption Eve Special services at churches throughout Vienna

8 PM — Gala Concert Burgtheater | Fräulein Lindqvist to perform

AUGUST 15 — FEAST OF THE ASSUMPTION

All Day — Holy Day celebrations Processions, masses, and festivities throughout Vienna

Tickets may be obtained at theatre box offices or through your hotel concierge.

For society events, introductions may be arranged through the appropriate channels.
