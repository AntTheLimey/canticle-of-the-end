Backerstrasse 14 Adler Rooms

testimonial high (direct testimony from Adler's victim) Anna_Lindqvist reveals to the party once she can speak

Description

An address in Vienna: Bäckerstraße 14. Personal rooms of Kapellmeister Friedrich Adler. Known to Anna_Lindqvist because she has been brought there for “training sessions” during her captivity-in-plain-sight.

What It Reveals

  • Adler has a private Vienna base distinct from the University and the Conservatory. This is where he stores personal effects — possibly correspondence with Herzfeld, the Brotherhood_of_the_Open_Measure’s Munich contacts via Der_Kantor, and notes on the Engine’s performance requirements.
  • The address is not widely known. Kaunitz may or may not know it. Vogel probably does not — the rooms are Adler’s personal space, not Brotherhood operational property.
  • The rooms are almost certainly unoccupied during the masquerade (Adler is at the Palais Lobkowitz) and remain unoccupied afterwards because Adler is fleeing.

Where Found

Bäckerstrasse is a narrow medieval street in the Vienna inner city, between St. Stephen’s Cathedral district and the university quarter. Number 14 is a townhouse with rented upper-floor rooms typical for professional men of Adler’s standing.

Connections

  • Discovery vector: Anna_Lindqvist (Session 9 debrief, once she can speak)
  • Actionable by: the entire party — any PC with an hour to spare before the political noose tightens
  • Access method: a lock (Katherine_Ward can pick it; Nell_Coker can case the building first) or direct entry if the rooms have been abandoned during Adler’s flight
  • Ownership confusion: if Adler flees to the University rather than Bäckerstrasse, the rooms are a soft target. If he is captured or killed, the rooms are unguarded.

Relationships

  • Reveals location of Anton Adler — Adler's personal rooms in Vienna
  • Testified by Anna Lindqvist — Anna knows the address from her training sessions with Adler
  • Actionable in Session 9 Plan — Available as an investigation lead in Session 9 once Anna is debriefed
