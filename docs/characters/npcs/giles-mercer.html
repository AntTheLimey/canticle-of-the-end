Overview

Giles Mercer was an Order of St. Aelfric investigator assigned to monitor occult activity beneath the stage at an unnamed London theater. During his investigation, he discovered and encountered a minor cult ritual—and was killed or permanently incapacitated in the encounter.

The monologue he recited before his death provided the initial clue that led to the discovery of the Aeternum Choir’s existence.

Status

Mercer is deceased as of the campaign start. His final moments and the intelligence he gathered before death serve as the mission briefing hook for the investigators.

Key Information

  • His monologue fragment pointed to the existence of the Canticle
  • His death confirmed the presence of serious supernatural threat
  • His fate motivated the Order to dispatch Rooke and Bellamy

Relationship to Campaign

Mercer serves primarily as a plot catalyst rather than an active NPC. His death and the intelligence from it launches the entire Chapter 1 investigation. He represents the cost of underestimating the cult’s power and organization.
