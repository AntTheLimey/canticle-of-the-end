Summary

Pre-dawn assault on the Sealed_Anatomical_Theatre beneath the University_of_Vienna, conducted by a combined force of investigators, Sardinian mercenaries, and Russian operatives. Two teams breached simultaneously: a rescue team (Adrien, Katherine, Nell, Ferrante’s mercenaries) through the Roman tunnels and west wall door, and a Russian team (Nikolai, Andrei, Sasha, Ivan) through the main approach from Herzfeld’s office. The assault culminated in the destruction of the Harmonic_Engine via Georgiana’s counter-ritual using the Command_Tuning_Fork, the rescue of Caroline_Hartley, and the capture of Albin_Herzfeld.

Context

The assault was planned at the War Council (Session 12) after intelligence confirmed the ritual timeline had shifted from August 15 to August 13. The party launched pre-dawn on 12 August — one day ahead of the ritual’s projected completion. By the time the assault teams entered the theatre, the ritual was active and at an advanced stage, with the Harmonic_Engine operational and Chorus Dead animating the tiered seating.

Participants

Rescue Team (west wall door):

  • Adrien_de_Montferrand — team lead, rifle shot that disrupted Herzfeld’s surgery
  • Katherine_Ward — medical intervention on Caroline
  • Nell_Coker — lockpick on the west wall door, precision pistol shot, fled during building shake
  • Capitano_Luca_Ferrante and Sardinian mercenaries — vanguard, Ferrante physically restrained Herzfeld

Main Team (office stairwell):

  • Emma_Wentworth — identified acoustic baffles, directed their destruction
  • Freddy_Cavendish — helped topple baffles despite amnesia episode
  • Georgiana_Wentworth — performed counter-ritual via Command Tuning Fork
  • Varrio_Harrowmont — blind miraculous shot, later feral assault on Herzfeld
  • Thomas_Wyndham — fought Chorus Dead, toppled baffles, shot Varrio to stop feral assault

Russian Team:

  • Nikolai_Volkonsky, Major Andrei Volkonsky, Sasha, Ivan — breached from opposite side, engaged Chorus Dead, took custody of Herzfeld

Adversaries:

  • Albin_Herzfeld — cult leader, at the Engine performing surgery on Caroline
  • Student cultists — ambushed the rescue team at the west wall door (all killed)
  • Die_Chor_Leichen — reanimated dead filling the tiered seating, harmonic pressure weapon

Rescued:

  • Caroline_Hartley — extracted from surgical table, throat wound stitched by Katherine

Outcome & Consequences

Immediate:

  • Harmonic_Engine destroyed (putrefied and collapsed when counter-ritual succeeded)
  • Albin_Herzfeld captured by Russian operatives — faceless (Varrio’s feral attack), mummified in bandages
  • Caroline_Hartley rescued — throat wound stitched, voice status pending
  • Sealed_Anatomical_Theatre wrecked
  • All student cultists killed
  • Die_Chor_Leichen inert once Engine shut down

Casualties:

  • Varrio_Harrowmont — shot by Thomas, 1 HP, unconscious, SAN near zero
  • Thomas_Wyndham — SAN 60 → 56, nosebleeds and ear bleeds from harmonic pressure
  • Multiple SAN consequences across all PCs (amnesia, rage, mania, necrophobia, hysterics)
  • One Sardinian mercenary — serious neck wound from student ambush
  • One Sardinian — mental break during assault

Campaign-level:

  • Vienna is the fourth disrupted ritual site (London, Lyon, Venice, Vienna). The Aeternum_Choir needs 5 of 8 for the Grand Canticle.
  • The Brotherhood_of_the_Open_Measure’s Vienna cell is completely destroyed.
  • Georgiana_Wentworth permanently marked by the counter-ritual (white streak, iridescent skin, residual hum, fork bond permanent)
