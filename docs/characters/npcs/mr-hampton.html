Physical Description

A kindly-faced gentleman of sixty-five, fastidious in his dress and habits. His manner is warm and paternal, with a quiet attentiveness to detail that extends to every aspect of his household.

Background

Mr Hampton is a well-connected gentleman resident at the Hampton Residence on Marine Parade, Brighton. He and Mrs_Hampton never had children of their own, and they dote on the younger people in their circle. The Hamptons opened their home to Marina_Garrick and Georgiana_Dillwyn during the events of the Loom & Lucidity investigation in September 1812.
