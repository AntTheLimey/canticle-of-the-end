Cecile Vaillant

Role Translator, scholar, Order operative Nationality French Status alive Age 31
Role: Order of St.

Role: Order of St. Aelfric liaison in Paris; met the investigators at Le Lys de Marbre inn upon their arrival.

Key Functions:

  • Greeted the party at their hotel when they arrived in Paris (late June 1814)
  • Briefed party on Parisian society and political factions
  • Provided formal invitation to Hôtel de Brissac ball
  • Offered letters of introduction to Lyon contacts
  • Did NOT travel to Lyon

Campaign History

Chapter 2 — Paris Arrival

Cecile greeted the investigators at their hotel when they arrived in Paris on the journey from London to Lyon. She served as the Order’s point of contact, facilitating their entry into Parisian society and providing the connection to the Ball at the Hôtel de Brissac where they met Puyrault.
