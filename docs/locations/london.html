Description

The capital of the British Empire and setting for Chapter 1 of the campaign. The investigators begin their journey here, recruited by Lord_Percival_Harcourt into the Order_of_St_Aelfric. The London cell of the Aeternum_Choir — the Orphean_Society — is disrupted during Chapter 1.

Notable Features

  • Regency-era London during the Season of 1814
  • Home base of the Order_of_St_Aelfric
  • Site of the first Aeternum Choir cell disruption
