Freddy_Cavendish

The Honourable Frederick Cavendish, 22. Second son of an English noble family. Socially connected, he procured Masquerade invitations and masks for the party. Not a member of the Order of St. Aelfric, but has witnessed the supernatural firsthand — including the Nightgaunt attack on Palais Kinsky.

  • Player: Jay
  • Introduced: Session 6 (Vienna)
  • Replaced: Colonel_Henri_Moreau

Adrien_de_Montferrand

Viscount de Montferrand. French aristocrat and former cavalry officer who hosts the party at Palais_Kinsky in Vienna. Member of the Order_of_St_Aelfric, recruited during the Vienna chapter. Led the charge to the Polizeidirektion and delivered the killing blow to the Nightgaunt.

  • Player: Anna
  • Introduced: Vienna (Chapter 3)
  • Replaced: Marina_Garrick

Emma_Wentworth

Wiltshire gentlewoman, 17, from Tarryford. Theatre-obsessed after the Drury Lane incident. Courageous and curious in equal measure. Campaign-defining moment in Session 6: bit the Nightgaunt’s neck during combat, tearing away flesh. Subject of Graf Sternberg’s romantic pursuit; Thomas_Wyndham is in love with her.

  • Player: Missy
  • Introduced: The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1)
  • No character changes

Georgiana_Wentworth

Emma’s elder sister, 20. Gentlewoman researcher from Tarryford. Sharp-minded and composed under pressure. Drew swords in her nightgown during the Nightgaunt attack. Identified the Nightgaunt from the Revelations_of_Glaaki.

  • Player: Beth
  • Introduced: The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1)
  • No character changes
  • Phobias: Theatre (Drury Lane, Chapter 0.5), Fish (Chapter 0.6)

Katherine_Ward

Real name: Katherine Langley, alias “the Rook.” Age 31. Spy and Order_of_St_Aelfric operative. Cover identity: widow of Captain Edward Ward (Royal Navy), working as a companion for young ladies. Trained thief and infiltrator. Encountered a Hound_of_Tindalos at St. Margaret’s Academy and contained it by disrupting angles.

  • Player: Juel
  • Introduced: Session 7 (Vienna)
  • Replaced: Charlotte_Thorne
  • Character files: Katherine_Ward_Sheet, Katherine_Ward_Background

Varrio_Harrowmont

Dr. Septimus Harrowmont. Order_of_St_Aelfric consulting surgeon. Disrupted the Venice cell (Confraternita_del_Bel_Canto) before joining the main party in Vienna. Captured in Session 5 near the Graben, extracted via Dr. Fischbein’s legal intervention. Killed Brenner in Session 2 after Brenner revealed the Harmonic Engine.

  • Player: Phil
  • Introduced: Vienna (Chapter 3), Session 2
  • Previous characters: Jacob, Commissaire Delaroche

Keeper NPCs (Former PCs)

Thomas_Wyndham

British Army officer (Light Dragoons) from London. Loyal, volatile, deeply in love with Emma_Wentworth. Keeper NPC throughout the campaign. Maintained armed vigil outside Emma’s room after the Nightgaunt attack. Subject of the Sternberg duel subplot.

Charlotte_Thorne

Northumberland heiress, archaeologist, author. Steady, perceptive, deeply skeptical of the uncanny. Previously played by Juel; retired in Session 7 after severe Nightgaunt wounds. Convalescing at the party’s lodgings.
