Physical Description

A striking Frenchwoman who always wears a heavy riding glove on her left hand, concealing disfigurement from torture during the Revolution. Her bearing is sharp and self-possessed, with an intensity that marks her out in any company.

Background

Mlle Dumain fled France after the Reign of Terror, carrying with her the scientific training of a former student of Antoine Lavoisier. A genius inventor and committed radical, she designed and built the Air Loom alongside Captain_King. More willing to resort to violence than King, she is the technical mind behind the Gang’s operations and its most dangerous member.
