Chapter 2 Overview

2

Overview

The road south from London led through Paris — a ball at the Hôtel de Brissac, a chance encounter with Comte de Puyrault — and into Lyon, where the Société Harmonique de l’Aube under Mathilde_Savarin operated a network of surgically altered children, tunnel rituals, and political terror. Four PCs died in Lyon. The chapter ended with Marina_Garrick detonating herself and the Chakota in the tunnels beneath the Silkweavers_Guild.

Key NPCs

  • Mathilde_Savarin — cult leader
  • Abbe_Etienne_Duplessis — Order contact in Lyon
  • Colonel_Etienne_Moreau — military ally, joined the party
  • Commissaire_Jules_Delaroche — Lyon police chief, compromised by Savarin
  • Emeric_de_Puyrault — nobleman, offered lodgings at Le_Coteau_des_Ombres
  • Lucien_Goupil — hosted the masquerade at Maison_du_Corbeau
  • Marquise_Eloise_de_Camberonne — hosted the ball at the Château
  • Abbe_Ferrant — Savarin’s fanatic priest
  • Doctor_Emile_Carreau — surgeon operating on children
  • Luc_Fouchard — tunnel guard

Key Locations

  • Hotel_de_Brissac — Paris ball
  • Le_Coteau_des_Ombres — Puyrault estate, party lodgings
  • Maison_du_Corbeau — Goupil’s masquerade, secret ritual witnessed
  • Hotel_de_Camberonne — Marquise’s ball, key alliances formed
  • Hopital_de_la_Charite — Orphans’ Hospital, raid to free Delaroche’s children
  • Fourviere — Roman temple excavation beneath the hill
  • Silkweavers_Guild — final assault, Marina’s death

Sessions

Chapter 2 was played at the table but individual session transcripts were not preserved. The full narrative is recorded in the chapter wrap-up.

Threads & Hooks

  • The masquerade at Maison_du_Corbeau — the investigators witnessed a Formless_Spawn summoning and a child sacrifice, and did not intervene
  • Augustus_Bolt killed the Choir Below in the Orphean sub-basement — a mercy that haunted the party
  • Marina’s death and the intelligence recovered from Savarin’s headquarters pointed to Albin_Herzfeld in Vienna
  • Colonel_Etienne_Moreau survived and joined the party for Vienna
