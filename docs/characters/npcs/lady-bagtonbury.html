“Elderly Dowager Duchess and her lapdog (Nero).”

Background

Lady Bagtonbury is an elderly dowager duchess encountered in Brighton society during the Loom & Lucidity investigation in spring 1813. She is invariably accompanied by her lapdog, Nero, and commands the deference her rank requires.
