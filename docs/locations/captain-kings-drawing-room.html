Overview

The private drawing room of Captain_King, a Royal Navy officer residing in Brighton. The room served as the setting for an intimate Regency soirée — card games, polite conversation, and the careful exchange of social intelligence under the surface pleasantries. The atmosphere carries the particular tension of a naval officer’s household: ordered, watchful, and conscious of rank.

Campaign Significance

Scene of a key social encounter during Chapter 0.1, featuring card play, hidden observers, and undercurrents of intrigue. The soirée here was a significant moment in the Loom & Lucidity investigation.
