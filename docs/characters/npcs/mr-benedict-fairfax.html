Physical Description

Broad-shouldered, carries himself with a soldier’s discipline. A deep scar runs across his left cheek. Age 52. Keeps all belongings meticulously clean and organised.

Background

Mr. Benedict Fairfax is a retired military officer serving the Order_of_St_Aelfric as Weapons Master and Survival Trainer at Ravenwood. He provides tactical advice, field strategy, and combat training when Order members must engage in direct action.

Measured, direct, with an undercurrent of dry humour. Speaks in clipped commands. Deep scorn for anything occult — calls it “coward’s magic.” Only trusts those who’ve seen real blood. Carries guilt from prior failed missions, possibly involving fallen Order agents.

Roleplaying Fairfax

Treats investigators like soldiers — respect earned, not given. Challenges anyone who panics or hesitates. Offers practical advice before missions; insists they check gear. If provoked, will reveal a traumatic memory or scar from previous encounters with Mythos creatures.
