ACTIVE PLAYER CHARACTERS (Vienna, August 1814)

Jay (Juel’s Husband)

CURRENT: Freddy Cavendish

  • The Honourable Frederick Cavendish, 22. Second son of an English noble family.
  • Introduced: Session 6 (Vienna), replacing Colonel Henri Moreau.
  • Status: Active PC. Socially connected, produced Masquerade invitations and masks.
  • NOT a member of the Order of St. Aelfric. Witnessed the Nightgaunt attack on Palais Kinsky.
  • Partially briefed on the supernatural threat.

Previous Characters:

  • Colonel Henri Moreau — Order operative, French military officer.
    • Played: Late Lyon (Chapter 2) through Vienna Session 5.
    • DIED: Session 5, August 7, 1814. Assassinated by the Bauer brothers in a street ambush near the Graben. Three stab wounds. First PC death of the Vienna chapter. Body recovered to the Josephinum.
  • Augustus Bolt — Explorer, map-maker, rationalist. Groomsman at the Huntley wedding, Order recruit.
    • Played: London (Chapter 1) through Lyon (Chapter 2).
    • DIED: Lyon, during the Orphans’ Hospital raid. Killed in the assault on Savarin’s operation.
  • James_Bennet — Gentleman poet, 22, from Tarryford, Wiltshire. Pre-gen from the Regency Cthulhu Investigator Pack.
    • Played: The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1). Jay’s first character.
    • DIED: Autumn 1813, consumed in the Long Corridor at Northlake Hall, Tarryford. First PC death of the campaign.

Anna (Ant’s Wife)

CURRENT: Adrien de Montferrand (Viscount)

  • French aristocrat, Viscount. Hosts the party at Palais Kinsky in Vienna.
  • Introduced: Vienna (Chapter 3), replacing Marina Garrick.
  • Status: Active PC. Order of St. Aelfric member (recruited in Vienna). Led the charge to the Polizeidirektion. Delivered the killing blow to the Nightgaunt.

Previous Characters:

  • Marina Garrick — Surrey gentlewoman, aspiring investigator, survivor of Osney Grange.
    • Played: Loom & Lucidity (Chapter 0.1, solo) through Lyon (Chapter 2). Original protagonist of the campaign’s earliest solo sessions.
    • DIED: Lyon (Chapter 2). Circumstances to be confirmed with Keeper.
    • Her investigation notebook (containing notes from London and Lyon) was confiscated by the Polizeidirektion during the Palais Kinsky room search on August 6 (Session 4), compromising the party’s operational security.

Missy

CURRENT: Emma Wentworth

  • Wiltshire gentlewoman, 17, from Tarryford. Pre-gen from the Regency Cthulhu Investigator Pack. Theatre-obsessed after the Drury Lane incident (London). Courageous and curious.
  • Played since: The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1). No character changes.
  • Status: Active PC. Campaign-defining moment in Session 6: bit the Nightgaunt’s neck during combat, tearing away flesh. Subject of Graf Sternberg’s romantic pursuit. Thomas Wyndham is in love with her.

Beth (Phil’s wife)

CURRENT: Georgiana Wentworth

  • Emma’s sister, 20, gentlewoman researcher from Tarryford. Pre-gen from the Regency Cthulhu Investigator Pack. Sharp-minded, carries phobias of theatre and fish after The Long Corridor.
  • Played since: The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1). No character changes.
  • Status: Active PC. Tactical combatant. Drew swords in her nightgown during the Nightgaunt attack. Identified the Nightgaunt from the Revelations of Gla’aki.
  • Phobias: Theatre (Drury Lane, Chapter 0.5), Fish (river/siren’s cave, The Viscount Who Loves Me, Chapter 0.6).

Juel (Jay’s wife)

CURRENT: Katherine Ward (Mrs. Katherine Ward)

  • Real name: Katherine Langley, alias “the Rook.” Age 31. Spy. Order of St. Aelfric member.
  • Cover identity: Widow of Captain Edward Ward (Royal Navy). Works as a companion/chaperone for young ladies.
  • Introduced: Session 7 (Vienna). Dispatched by Lord Harcourt to join the party directly after receiving too many notes from the investigators.
  • Status: Active PC. Trained thief and infiltrator. Encountered a Hound of Tindalos at St. Margaret’s Academy (contained it by disrupting angles). Recruited by the Order after that incident.
  • Background: Lower-class, charity-educated. Mentored by Jonathan Pike (“Jon”), a locksmith-turned-broker who manages her professional identity. Moral code: will not harm innocents, will not take jobs involving children or coercion, violence only in self-defense.
  • Character files: Katherine_Ward_Sheet, Katherine_Ward_Background

Previous Characters:

  • Charlotte Thorne — Northumberland heiress, archaeologist, author (writes under a male pseudonym). Steady, perceptive, deeply skeptical of the uncanny.
    • Played: The Viscount Who Loves Me (Chapter 0.6) through Vienna Session 6.
    • RETIRED: Session 7, August 8, 1814. Too severely wounded by the Nightgaunt (three-clawed slash across shoulder) to continue field operations. Wrote retirement letters to Harcourt and Honoria. Now a Keeper NPC, convalescing at the party’s lodgings.
  • Jane_Radcliffe — Con artist, 23. Real name Maggie Rowlins. Pre-gen from the Regency Cthulhu Investigator Pack.
    • Played: The Long Corridor (Chapter 0.1) through The Scandal Beneath the Stage (Chapter 0.5). Juel’s first character.
    • DIED: December 1813, swallowed by the theatre during the Drury Lane incident (Chapter 0.5).

Phil (Beth’s husband)

CURRENT: Varrio Harrowmont (Dr. Septimus Harrowmont)

  • Order of St. Aelfric consulting surgeon. Disrupted the Venice cell (Confraternita del Bel Canto) off-camera in Chapter 2.5 before joining the main party in Vienna.
  • Introduced to the table: Vienna (Chapter 3), Session 2 onward.
  • Status: Active PC. Captured in Session 5 (ambush near the Graben), extracted in Session 6 via Dr. Fischbein’s legal intervention. PTSD from witnessing Moreau’s death — fainted at the sight of the Nightgaunt. Killed Brenner in Session 2 (broke his neck) after Brenner revealed the Harmonic Engine.

Previous Characters:

  • Monsieur Delaroche — Chief of Police, Lyon. Phil took over Delaroche after Jacob’s death.
    • Played: Lyon (Chapter 2).
    • DIED: Lyon (Chapter 2). Killed during the assault on the old Silkweavers’ Guild.
  • Jacob — Ravenwood House coachman, former military (served in Ireland during the Rebellion). Phil joined the campaign in Chapter 2 by taking over this existing NPC.
    • Played: Lyon (Chapter 2). Phil’s first character.
    • DIED: Lyon (Chapter 2). Killed during the Orphans’ Hospital raid, before Phil took over Delaroche.

Elizabeth (Ant’s daughter)

CURRENT: Inactive (one-time appearance)

Previous Characters:

  • Lady Georgiana Dillwyn — Gentlewoman, fellow investigator with Marina Garrick.
    • Played: Loom & Lucidity (Chapter 0.1, Spring 1813). Elizabeth’s only appearance.
    • Status: UNKNOWN. Georgiana’s fate after Brighton is unrecorded.

KEEPER NPCs (Not Player Characters)

Thomas Wyndham

  • British infantry officer from London. Loyal, volatile, deeply in love with Emma Wentworth.
  • KEEPER NPC throughout the campaign. Controlled by the Keeper, not a player.
  • Maintained armed vigil outside Emma’s room after the Nightgaunt attack (Session 6). Identified three Brotherhood watchers at Palais Kinsky.
  • Subject of the Sternberg_Duel_Subplot — Graf Sternberg’s pursuit of Emma is pushing Thomas toward a formal challenge.

Charlotte Thorne (Retired PC — Session 7 onward)

  • Northumberland heiress. Steady, perceptive, deeply skeptical of the uncanny. Original investigator from London.
  • Previously played by Juel. Now a Keeper NPC, too wounded to participate in field operations.
  • Convalescing at the party’s lodgings following Nightgaunt wounds (Session 6). Wrote retirement letters to Harcourt and Honoria.
