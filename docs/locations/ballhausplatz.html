Description

The administrative heart of the Habsburg Empire. The Ballhausplatz houses the State Chancellery (Staatskanzlei), where Prince Metternich and the Austrian diplomatic apparatus conduct the business of empire. The Geheimpolizei and the emerging Congress_of_Vienna administration both operate from this complex.

Notable Features

  • State Chancellery (Metternich’s headquarters)
  • Geheimpolizei offices
  • Congress preparatory administration
  • Heavy security presence; courier traffic at all hours

Relationships

  • Part of Vienna Overview — Located in central Vienna, the Innere Stadt
  • Headquarters of Geheimpolizei — The Geheimpolizei operates from Ballhausplatz
  • Headquarters of Congress of Vienna — The Congress of Vienna administration is centred at Ballhausplatz
