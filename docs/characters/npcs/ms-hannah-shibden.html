Physical Description

A woman of about forty-five, more restrained and stern in manner than her companion Ms. Cooper. Carries a small leather-bound notebook in which she records parish intrigues with methodical precision.

Background

Hannah Shibden is the more calculating half of Osney Grange’s resident gossip partnership. Where Amelia Cooper is garrulous and indiscriminate, Shibden is restrained and strategic, keeping detailed notes on the intrigues of the parish in a small leather-bound notebook. She is not intentionally malicious, but her methodical approach to gathering and cataloguing information makes her a formidable source of local intelligence. She lives with Ms. Cooper in the centre of the village.
