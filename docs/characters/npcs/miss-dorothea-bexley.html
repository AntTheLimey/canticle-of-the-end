Background

Miss Dorothea Bexley is an occultist and symbologist attached to the Order_of_St_Aelfric, resident at Hartwell_House. Older than most at the table, with hair pinned in a precise chignon and dressed in dark, austere clothing. She specialises in occult symbols and their origins, and is often consulted for her knowledge of esoteric rites and sigils.

Severe, sharp-eyed, dislikes nonsense. Speaks rarely but precisely.

Campaign History

  • Winter 1813 (Training Montage): One of six instructors who trained the investigators at Hartwell_House during the winter between The Long Corridor and The Scandal Beneath the Stage.

Campaign History
