Background

Sir Andrew Duncan is a junior government minister encountered in Brighton society during the Loom & Lucidity investigation in spring 1813. Politically ambitious and carefully spoken, he navigates social occasions with the practised caution of a man whose career depends on saying the right thing to the right people.
